<Callout kind="info" title="Prerequisites">
Before starting, ensure you have:
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- A valid email address for account verification
- Internet connection for accessing `https://app.sivakumar-s.com` (replace with your instance URL if self-hosted)
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to register and log in to SIVAKUMAR S.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Sign-Up Page" icon="globe">
    Navigate to the SIVAKUMAR S homepage at `https://app.sivakumar-s.com/signup`.

    Click the **Sign Up** button prominently displayed on the landing page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Fill out the registration form:

    | Field       | Description                  |
    |-------------|------------------------------|
    | Email       | Your work email address     |
    | Password    | Create a strong password    |
    | Full Name   | Your display name           |

    Agree to the terms and click **Create Account**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Your Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from SIVAKUMAR S.

    Click the verification link to activate your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to `https://app.sivakumar-s.com/login`.

    Enter your email and password, then click **Log In**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
Save your credentials securely. Consider enabling two-factor authentication in your account settings after setup.
</Callout>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Once logged in, you land on the dashboard. Create a space for your project docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
    Click **New Space** in the top-right corner or the **+** button on the dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings">
    Enter space details:

    - **Name**: e.g., "NS Multispeciality Hospital Docs"
    - **Description**: Brief overview of the space
    - **Visibility**: Public, Private, or Workspace

    Click **Create Space**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add and Edit Your First Document

Spaces contain documents written in MDX for rich, interactive content.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Document" icon="file-text">
    Inside your new space, click **New Document**.

    Name it "Welcome" or "Introduction".
  </Step>

  <Step title="Edit Content" icon="edit-3">
    Use the editor to add content. Here's a starter MDX example:

````mdx
---
title: Welcome to My Docs
description: Your project documentation starts here.
---

## Overview

Welcome! This is your first document.
````

    Preview changes in real-time and click **Publish** when ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Visual Editor" icon="edit">
    Drag-and-drop components like images and callouts for non-coders.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Markdown Mode" icon="code">
    Write raw MDX for full control:

````markdown
<Callout kind="success">Your content here!</Callout>
````

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Basic Interface Tour

<Expandable title="Dashboard Overview" default-open="true">
The dashboard shows your spaces and recent activity. Key sections:

- **Spaces List**: Access all your documentation spaces
- **Quick Actions**: New space, document, or invite team members
- **Activity Feed**: Recent changes and notifications

Navigate using the left sidebar for spaces and the top bar for search and user menu.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Editor Features">
Advanced editor tools include:
- Live preview
- Component palette
- Version history
- Collaboration (real-time editing)
</Expandable>
