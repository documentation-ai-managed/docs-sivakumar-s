## How to Upgrade

Follow these steps to update your SIVAKUMAR S documentation to the latest version.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Current Version" icon="package">
    View your current version in the dashboard at `https://dashboard.example.com/settings`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Backup Your Docs" icon="database-backup">
    Export your MDX files to avoid data loss.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run Update" icon="download">
    <CodeGroup tabs="npm,yarn">
````bash
npm update sivakumar-s-docs
````
````bash
yarn upgrade sivakumar-s-docs
````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Changes" icon="check-circle">
    Test your pages for parsing errors and review the changelog.
  </Step>
</Steps>
